Places to Visit

Devi Talab Mandir

Devi Talab Mandir is a 200-year-old Hindu temple located in Jalandhar, Punjab. The main goddess is Durga, and it is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas in India.

Jang-e-Azadi Memorial

Jang-e-Azadi is a recently developed museum as a memorial for all the Punjabis who sacrificed their lives during the Indian Independence.

Pushpa Gujral Science City

Science City is a haven for science enthusiasts with motto to help people develop an interest in the working of the physical world around them.

Wonderland

Wonderland is an amusement and water park which functions as a conjuncture between rides, waterpark, dining hall, entertainment and shopping centre.

Rangla Punjab Haveli

Rangla Punjab Haveli in Jalandhar is a centre where the culture, traditional lifestyle, food and various art forms of Punjab are showcased.

Sodal Mandir

Sodal Mandir is a temple located in Jalandhar, Punjab. The temple's deity is Baba Sodal and is worshipped by over millions of Pilgrims.

Curo Highstreet

Curo High Street, is a European style high-street market place, offering the comforts of a mall providing clothing shops, food court and PVR.

Guru Gobind Singh Stadium

Guru Gobind Singh Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Jalandhar, India. It is currently used for football matches. The stadium holds 22,000 people.

Nehru Park

The Company Bagh, formerly known as the Nehru Park, had its name changed after Independence. To this day, it continues to be one of the oldest parks.

Transportation Services

Adampur Airport is located 6.7 km from Adampur town in Jalandhar district, 28 km from Jalandhar and 27 km from Hoshiarpur. It just beside NH-3. As it lies just between the cities of Jalandhar and Hoshiarpur, it serves both the cities. Thus having a well connected transportation system to both the cities. People can travel via road using highways and travel to different places by trains from the railway station of the cities.
